About Me

Hi, I'm Ojas Tripathi

Makerspace Community Making Lead  ·  Research Technologist  ·  Ashoka University

I am a research-oriented technologist with a strong foundation in prototyping, interdisciplinary experimentation, and applied AI/ML development. My work in the Digital Makerspace and Human Immunology Lab at Ashoka University has involved designing and fabricating laboratory systems, leading technical teams, and developing data-driven solutions for clinical applications such as flow cytometry. I thrive at the intersection of engineering and research — where ideas transition from concept to tangible, functional prototypes — and am motivated by opportunities that merge innovation with meaningful scientific or technological impact.
